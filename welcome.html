ABOUT THIS COURSE

What this is

AI For Work Bootcamp is a beginner-friendly course that teaches you simple, practical AI workflows you can use in real work and everyday tasks.

This is a beta — meaning you are one of the first people to go through it. The content is real and complete, but some things may be rough around the edges. Your feedback helps make it better.

THE COURSE

What you will do

This course has 5 short levels. Each one builds on the last and ends with something practical you keep.

1

Find Your AI Starting Point

You will pick one real task where AI could help you.

2

Build a Better Work Prompt

You will write your first structured AI message using a simple framework.

3

Research Without Blind Trust

You will learn how to check AI responses so you use them safely.

4

Map a Workflow AI Can Help With

You will map out the steps of a task AI can support.

5

Build Your Personal AI Workflow

You will put everything together into one workflow you can actually use.

WHAT YOU WALK AWAY WITH

What you will have at the end

  • One practical AI use case that fits your real work One starter prompt or message you have already written One habit or checklist for checking AI responses One repeatable workflow you can use again and again

COMPLETION

How to be counted as a completer

To complete the beta, you need to do three things:

1

Finish all 5 levels

2

Submit your final workflow using the form at the end of Level 5

3

Fill out the short feedback form after you submit your workflow

That is it. No test. No certificate. Just a workflow you actually built.

HELP

If you get stuck

Use the support form linked at the bottom of each level page. Describe where you got stuck and what you were trying to do. The founder reviews every submission and will follow up.


There is no live chat or email support during the beta. The form is the best way to reach us.

How to host this page for free using Google Drive

  1. Save this file (welcome.html) to your computer.
  2. Go to drive.google.com and upload the file.
  3. Right-click the file in Google Drive and click Share.
  4. Change access to Anyone with the link can view.
  5. Copy the file's sharing link. It will look like: docs.google.com/file/d/XXXXX/view
  6. Change the word view at the end to preview so it reads: docs.google.com/file/d/XXXXX/preview — this makes it render as a page, not a download.
  7. That URL is your Welcome Page link. Use it anywhere you would have linked to a Systeme.io page.

How to link this page from Systeme.io

  1. In your Systeme.io course, open the Welcome & Start Here lecture.
  2. Delete everything currently in the lecture editor.
  3. Add a single Text block that says: Click here to open your Welcome Page.
  4. Link that text to the Google Drive preview URL above.
  5. Save. The lecture now acts as a redirect to your fully branded page.
